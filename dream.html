My Story
Who I am & where I'm going

My name is written in the stars above America

I was born with a dream too big for borders. From the moment I first saw those gleaming skyscrapers in photos, those wide open highways stretching to the horizon — I knew. America was calling my name.

This is my private sanctuary. Every thought I record here, every dream I trace, every goal I set — it is all fuel for the journey ahead. I am not just dreaming of America. I am preparing for it. Every single day.

One day I will stand on American soil and look back at this page and smile. Because it all started right here.

Entry 001 · The Beginning
The night I decided America was my destination
It was not a dramatic moment. No fireworks, no revelation. I simply sat down one evening and wrote in my notebook: I am going to America. And the moment I wrote it, it became real. It stopped being a wish and became a plan. That night changed everything.
Entry 002 · On Longing
Why America? This is my honest answer.
People ask me why America specifically. I struggle to explain it fully. It is partly the culture, partly the opportunity, partly the scale of possibility. But mostly it is a feeling — that somewhere across that ocean there is a life that fits me perfectly. A city that will feel like home the moment I arrive. A community I have not met yet but already belong to.
Entry 003 · The Hard Days
When the dream feels far away
Some days the dream feels impossibly distant. Visa processes, finances, logistics — the practical reality can be crushing. On those days I come back to this page. I read what I have written. I look at the city cards and the lucid dream notes. And I remember: the distance between a dream and reality is called work. And I am willing to do the work.
Entry 004 · A Promise
A letter to my future self in America
Dear future me — you made it. You are reading this from somewhere in America. You kept your promise. You did not give up when it was hard. You did not let fear win. You are living the life that the person who built this website only dreamed about. I am proud of you. You deserve every single moment of this.
